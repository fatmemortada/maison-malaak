About

Meet Malak

My name is Malak, founder of Maison Malaak and an eye beauty specialist with over 8 years of experience. As a certified esthetician and permanent makeup artist, I am dedicated to revealing each woman’s natural beauty through refined, precise, and personalized treatments.

Passionate about aesthetics, wellness, and the art of detail, I created Maison Malaak as a space where elegance, softness, and expertise come together. Every treatment is designed to offer a calming, high-end experience while enhancing the natural harmony of each client’s features with subtlety and care.

My mission is simple: to help you feel beautiful, confident, and radiant in a warm atmosphere where every client feels heard, valued, and truly cared for.
